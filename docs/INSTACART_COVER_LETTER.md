## Understanding the Advertiser-Customer Balance

What excites me most about this role is the opportunity to design advertising experiences that enhance rather than interrupt the shopping journey. Through my meal planning app, I've navigated similar complexity: how do you present recipe suggestions (analogous to sponsored content) in ways that feel helpful rather than promotional? How do you balance business metrics like conversion rates with customer satisfaction and trust?

My approach centered on:

- **Context-aware placement**: Surfacing recipes when users actively seek meal inspiration, not as interruptions
- **Personalization through data**: Using pantry inventory and dietary preferences to increase relevance
- **Performance measurement**: Tracking engagement metrics, conversion to grocery orders, and user retention
- **Iterative refinement**: A/B testing placement strategies and presentation formats

These same principles apply directly to designing ad experiences that feel native and valuable within the Instacart shopping flow.
